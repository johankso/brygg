1.8 kg Muntons dark malt extract:
"Chocolate malt, crystal malt and pale malt is used in the manufacture of Muntons dark malt extract."
10:1:0.5 basert på Brent's Brown Ale. Høres ut som mye pale malt.

2 kg Muntons dark spraymalt:
"A superb addition to any Stout, Porter or Brown Ale. Dark Spraymalt will improve the body, adding extra rich malt flavour and natural colour."



http://learn.kegerator.com/american-brown-ale/

Korn:
80% two-row pale/mild malt
10% krystallmalt
10% spesialmalt

OG (etter kok): 1.061

Fjos: 1.8 (med væske) + 2 kg (uten væske) ekstrakt (Fjos) = 3.8 kg
Konvertering: 1 pound grain = .75 pounds LME = .6 pounds DME. (http://www.homebrewtalk.com/showthread.php?t=128206)
Fjosnisse: 1.8/0.75 + 2/0.6 = (2.4 + 3.3) kg = 5.7

Pale: 5.7 * 0.8 = 4.6 kg
Krystall: 5.7 * 0.1 = 0.6 kg
Choco: 5.7 * 0.1 = 0.6 kg

Mesketemp: 67*C
